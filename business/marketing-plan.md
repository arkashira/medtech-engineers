# marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning Statement:**  
"MedTech Engineers: Your dedicated platform for pre-trained, full-stack software engineers specializing in medical devices and healthtech."

**Alternative Positioning Statements:**  
1. "Empowering healthtech startups with expert engineers in embedded systems and cloud solutions."
2. "Bridging the gap in medical device development with high-quality, full-stack engineering talent."
3. "Transforming healthtech innovation through specialized engineering expertise from embedded to cloud."

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
1. **Persona 1: Dr. Sarah Thompson**  
   - **Daily Job:** CTO of a healthtech startup focused on developing wearable medical devices.  
   - **Budget:** $150,000 annually for engineering talent.  

2. **Persona 2: Mark Johnson**  
   - **Daily Job:** Product Manager at a medical device company, overseeing the development of a new diagnostic tool.  
   - **Budget:** $100,000 annually for software development resources.  

3. **Persona 3: Emily Chen**  
   - **Daily Job:** Founder of a startup creating telehealth solutions, looking for engineers to integrate software with hardware.  
   - **Budget:** $120,000 annually for full-stack engineering support.  

## Channels

   - **CAC Estimate:** $300 per acquired customer.  

2. **Industry Conferences and Trade Shows**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $500 per acquired customer (includes booth costs and promotional materials).  

3. **Content Marketing (Webinars and Blogs)**

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize product features and pricing strategy; initiate pre-launch marketing campaign.
- **D-0:** Official product launch; host a launch webinar and release promotional content.
- **D+30:** Evaluate initial customer feedback; adjust marketing strategies based on performance metrics.
- **D+90:** Review success metrics; aim for 100 DAU and $50,000 in MRR.

## Success Metrics
- **Daily Active Users (DAU):** Target of 100 DAU by D+90.
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR):** Target of $50,000 by D+90.
