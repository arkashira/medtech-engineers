### Strategic Highlights

1. **Differentiation** – Unlike generic freelance platforms, medtech‑engineers offers *pre‑validated, regulatory‑ready* engineers and a *full‑stack, end‑to‑end* service model tailored to medical‑device constraints.
2. **Scalability** – Leveraging the Axentx BRAIN (pgvector) and production inference engines (vLLM) enables rapid scaling of both talent matching and software delivery pipelines.
3. **Risk Management** – Built‑in compliance checks, automated testing, and continuous monitoring reduce regulatory risk and accelerate approvals.
4. **Revenue Growth** – Subscription + project fees create recurring revenue, while revenue‑share models align incentives with client success.
